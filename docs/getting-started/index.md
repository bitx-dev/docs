# Getting Started 



## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 What is BitxPay?

BitxPay is a modern payment platform that enables businesses worldwide to **send, receive, convert, and settle money**—including both cryptocurrency and local fiat currencies—**quickly and securely**.

Unlike traditional bank transfers that can take days, BitxPay allows funds to move **within minutes**, operating **24/7**, including weekends and holidays.

With BitxPay’s **self-custodial wallet**, you remain fully in control of your funds. You hold your own private keys and manage your money securely without relying on third parties.

Think of BitxPay as a **global bridge between traditional finance and cryptocurrency**, empowering businesses to pay and get paid seamlessly across borders.

### 1.2 Vision & Mission

### Vision

To make global payments **fast, reliable, and accessible** for every business—regardless of location or currency.

### Mission

To build a payment platform where:

- Money moves **instantly**, not in days  
- Users **securely control their own funds**  
- **Blockchain technology** simplifies global transactions  
- Businesses can accept and settle payments in **both cryptocurrency and local currencies** without unnecessary delays or complexity  

BitxPay aims to **empower businesses to grow globally** without being limited by slow banking systems or complicated payment processes.

## 1.3 Target Audience

BitxPay is designed for:

- **Businesses of all sizes** — from small shops to large enterprises  
- **Payment processors** supporting merchant payments  
- **Banks and financial institutions** seeking to modernize treasury and settlement services  
- **Fintech companies and payment gateways** that require fast, global payout systems  
- **Anyone needing fast, secure, and compliant cross-border payments** using both cryptocurrency and traditional currency systems

# 2. Overview of BitxPay

BitxPay is designed to simplify how businesses and financial partners **send, receive, convert, and settle money** using **stablecoins, cryptocurrencies, and traditional fiat currencies** across the globe.

By combining blockchain technology with compliant financial infrastructure, BitxPay enables **fast, secure, and reliable transactions**, helping organizations move money globally with ease and confidence.

## 2.1 Core Features

Below are the main features that make BitxPay stand out:

---

### 2.1.1 Instant Settlement

BitxPay settles payments **in minutes, not days**.

Traditional banking systems often take several business days to complete cross-border transactions. BitxPay eliminates this delay by enabling **instant settlement**, operating **24/7**, including weekends and holidays.

Businesses no longer need to wait for banking hours or clearing cycles—money moves **fast, reliably, and globally**.

---

### 2.1.2 Multi-Chain Wallet Support

BitxPay supports **multiple blockchain networks**.

Instead of being locked into a single blockchain, BitxPay can send and receive payments across various networks. This allows the platform to automatically select the **fastest and most cost-efficient route** for each transaction.

Multi-chain support helps users avoid network congestion, reduce fees, and enables **flexible global transfers**.

---

### 2.1.3 Stablecoin & Fiat Conversions

BitxPay supports payments in **stablecoins**—cryptocurrencies designed to maintain a stable value (such as the US dollar)—and enables seamless conversion into **local fiat currencies**.

For example:

- A business receives payment in a stablecoin like **USDC**
- BitxPay instantly converts it into **local currency**
- The business receives fiat in their **bank account or wallet**

This makes stablecoin payments practical even for businesses that prefer traditional money.

---

### 2.1.4 Secure Self-Custodial Wallet

BitxPay uses a **self-custodial wallet model**, meaning users control their own private keys.

This ensures that:

- Only you can access your funds  
- No third party holds or controls your money  
- You remain in complete control at all times  

Self-custody enhances security and reduces the risks associated with custodial wallet systems.

---

### 2.1.5 Compliance & Regulatory Coverage

BitxPay is built to meet financial regulations in major global markets. It is registered as a **Money Services Business (MSB)** in the United States and licensed as a **Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP)** in the European Union.

This includes:

- Strict **KYC (Know Your Customer)** verification  
- Robust **AML (Anti-Money Laundering)** controls  
- Compatibility with banks and regulated financial partners  

Strong compliance enables businesses to use BitxPay confidently, even in highly regulated environments.

## 2.2 Supported Blockchain Networks

BitxPay operates on **six major blockchain networks**. Blockchain-based transfers enable **faster settlement and lower costs** compared to traditional payment rails.

For every transaction, BitxPay automatically selects the **optimal network** based on speed and transaction fees, ensuring transfers are **efficient, reliable, and predictable**.

---

## 2.3 Supported Fiat & Local Currencies

BitxPay supports conversion and payout in **25+ local currencies worldwide**.

This removes the need for complex foreign exchange systems or multiple payout providers. BitxPay manages currency conversion and local payouts **within a single platform**.

Supported payout currencies include **major and widely used global currencies**, allowing businesses to operate seamlessly across multiple markets.

---

## 2.4 High-Level Architecture

BitxPay leverages advanced blockchain technology behind the scenes, while keeping the user experience **simple and non-technical**.

At a high level, the process works as follows:

1. **Funding**  
   A business funds its BitxPay wallet using stablecoins or cryptocurrency.

2. **Settlement**  
   BitxPay routes and settles the transaction through the **best available blockchain network**.

3. **Conversion**  
   Stablecoins are converted into **local fiat currency**, if required.

4. **Payout**  
   The final amount is delivered to the recipient’s **wallet or bank account**.

This architecture is designed to be **seamless, fast, and transparent**, with no complex setup or technical steps required from the user.

# 3. Getting Started

BitxPay is built for simplicity, but as a **regulated global payment platform**, a few setup steps are required before you can begin using it.  
This section covers everything you need to get started **quickly and safely**.

---

## 3.1 Creating a BitxPay Account

To begin using BitxPay, you first need to create an account on the platform.

Typical steps include:

1. Visit **bitxpay.com** and select **“Open my Wallet”** or **“Request a Demo.”**
2. Enter a valid email address and create a **secure password**.
3. Confirm your email by clicking the verification link sent to your inbox.
4. Once verified, you’ll gain access to the **BitxPay dashboard**—your central hub for managing wallets and payments.

---

## 3.2 Account Types (Individual / Business / Enterprise)

BitxPay supports multiple account types depending on your usage needs:

### Individual Account
Best suited for individual users who want to send or receive payments in **cryptocurrency or stablecoins**.

### Business Account
Designed for **merchants and small businesses** that need to accept payments and settle funds efficiently.

### Enterprise Account
Tailored for **large companies, banks, payment processors, and fintech partners** requiring advanced features, higher limits, and custom settlement workflows.

Each account type unlocks features appropriate to its scale—from simple transfers to **global enterprise settlement operations**.

---

## 3.3 KYC & AML Requirements

As a regulated financial platform, BitxPay complies with global **Know Your Customer (KYC)** and **Anti-Money Laundering (AML)** regulations.

During verification, you may be asked to provide:

- Full legal name  
- Date of birth  
- Residential address  
- Government-issued identification (passport, national ID, or driver’s license)  
- Selfie or photo verification (in some cases)

These checks are standard for licensed payment providers and are required to unlock **full platform functionality**, including higher transaction limits and settlement access.

---

## 3.4 Logging In & Security Best Practices

Account security is critical. Follow these best practices to protect your BitxPay account:

- Use a **strong, unique password**
- Enable **two-factor authentication (2FA)** whenever possible
- Avoid logging in from **public computers or unsecured networks**
- Always verify emails and links to protect against phishing attacks

BitxPay applies **industry-standard security measures**, and combining them with good personal security habits ensures maximum protection.

---

## 3.5 Wallet Setup & Recovery

Once your account is created, you’ll have access to a **self-custodial wallet**, meaning **you control your private keys and funds**—not BitxPay or any third party.

To set up and secure your wallet:

1. **Generate your wallet keys** to gain secure access to your funds.
2. **Back up your recovery phrase** and store it offline in a safe location.
3. If you lose your device or forget your password, the **recovery phrase is the only way** to regain access.

Because BitxPay uses a self-custody model, you are fully responsible for safeguarding your recovery information.
