# Welcome to BITXpay

BITXpay is a cryptocurrency payment gateway. Accept Bitcoin, Ethereum, stablecoins, and 14 other assets with a single API, no node infrastructure, and real-time webhooks.

## Why BITXpay

### Move money in minutes
Send and receive payments 24/7 — no bank cut-off times, no weekends, no holidays.

### Self-custodial wallet
You hold your own keys. No third party controls your funds — full ownership, full security.

### Crypto meets local currency
Convert between crypto and local currencies seamlessly. Pay globally, settle locally.

## How it Works

1. **Create intent**: Amount, currency, recipient
2. **Show Checkout**: Hosted page or embedded widget
3. **Funds move**: Crypto and fiat, on-chain in minutes
4. **Webhook fires**: Payment confirmed with full receipt

## What you can build

### E-commerce checkout
Accept crypto and local currency payments on any storefront, embedded or hosted.

### Cross-border payments
Send money internationally in minutes. No correspondent banks, no 3-day delays.

### Global payroll
Pay contractors and employees worldwide in stablecoins or local currency, instantly.

### Multi-currency invoicing
Issue invoices in any currency with real-time rate lock-in and automatic settlement.
